## üé§ Script (Hindi)

### Opening (1 min)
```
"Namaste students! Aaj main aapko dikhaunga ki machines kitni 
smart ho sakti hain. Yeh hai IoT aur Robotics!"

"IoT matlab Internet of Things - jab normal cheezein smart 
ban jaati hain. Jaise smart AC, smart watch, Alexa."

"Robotics matlab machines jo khud decisions le sakti hain!"
```

### Drone Demo (5 min)
```
"Yeh hai hamara ESP Mini Drone!"

[Drone fly karke dikhao]

"Dekho - yeh automatically balance ho raha hai. Kaise? 
Isme gyroscope sensor hai jo har second check karta hai 
ki drone level hai ya nahi, aur motors ko adjust karta hai."

"Isko humne khud banaya hai - aur kal se aap bhi aise 
projects banana seekhenge!"
```

### Obstacle Car Demo (5 min)
```
"Ab dekhte hain yeh smart car!"

[Car table pe chalao]

"Main iske aage haath rakhunga..."

[Car turn leti hai]

"Dekha? Maine koi button nahi dabaya! Yeh car khud 
samajh gayi ki aage kuch hai. Kaise?"

"Isme ultrasonic sensor hai - jo sound waves bhejta hai 
aur wapas aane ka time count karta hai. Isse distance 
pata chal jaata hai."

"Yahi technology self-driving cars mein use hoti hai - 
Tesla, Waymo sab mein!"
```

### Gesture Car Demo (5 min)
```
"Ab sabse interesting project!"

[Remote haath mein lo]

"Yeh ek remote hai, aur yeh car. Dekhte hain..."

[Haath aage jhukao - car aage jaye]

"Dekha? Maine sirf haath jhukaya! Koi button nahi."

[Haath left/right tilt karo]

"Left tilt - car left! Right tilt - car right!"

"Isme MPU6050 sensor hai jo haath ka angle measure 
karta hai, aur wireless signal se car ko bhejta hai."

"Koi try karna chahega?"

[1-2 students ko try karwao]
```

### Closing (30 sec)
```
"Toh students, yeh tha IoT aur Robotics ka demo!"

"Kal se 3-din ka workshop shuru hoga jisme AAP log 
aise hi smart projects khud banayenge!"

"Excited ho? Kal milte hain!"
```
